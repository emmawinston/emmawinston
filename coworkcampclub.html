What's cowork.camp.club?

It's a...thing where campers team up on video chat (voice chat could work too, I think, and we have now also done it with text chat which is good for people with slow connections) and get stuff done. 'Stuff' can be whatever you want; as I'm writing this, four of us are co-working on (respectively) admin tasks, coding, writing, and fixing a broken modular synth.

If two or more of us are on friend camp at the same time and feel like co-working, we log on to Discord, open a group DM and start video chat. We spend a few minutes telling each other what we're going to work on, then one of us starts a timer (I normally do 25 or 30 minutes, but you decide - we've been using http://itsalmo.st lately for shared timers). When the time's up, we share what we did, we WARMLY CONGRATULATE each other (important!), and then repeat the process as many times as we like.

How do I use it?

Use the Discord link here to join: https://discord.gg/486CmUM

I picked Discord because you don't have to install anything but honestly I hate how overwhelming the UI is, so if you have any other suggestions, please pass them on to me, or Darius, or someone else.

  1. Before you get started, make sure your microphone and/or camera are set up in your user settings (bottom left, gear icon).
  2. You need to add anyone you want to work with as a friend before you can video or voice call them: right-click them in the sidebar of the general channel, and click Add Friend.
  3. To make a video call, go to your Friends page (Home button on top left > Friends) and click 'New Group DM' on the top right (the speech bubble with a + sign).
  4. Add whoever you want to call and click Create Group DM.
  5. Click the camera icon (or phone icon if you want voice only chat) on the top right.
  6. Do the thing!

Why?

I work from home, I'm not always very good at being a person, I get way more done when I'm accountable to other people, and Darius and I do this sometimes and enjoy it. I figured it would be nice to have a way for campers to do it together.

(If you're not a member of friend camp, but have somehow found this page, consider this me quietly exhorting you to make one of these with your pals rather than using ours - I'd love it if this stayed friend camp-only, sort of like a group chat.)
