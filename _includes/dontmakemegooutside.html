Don't Make Me Go Outside - playlist

This playlist is designed as a sampler of the early material discussed in my paper 'Don't Make Me Go Outside: Feminine melancholy, recovery, and rejecting resilience through electronic music'.

They were all made in bed, on an iPad mini in either Nanostudio or Korg Gadget, with the exception of The Waves, a slightly later song which was re-tracked (also in bed) on an OP-1 miniature synthesiser, my preferred device now that I'm more mobile and have disposable income again.

If you have further questions or comments about my talk or practice, please don't hesistate to get in touch at e.winston@gold.ac.uk. You can also find the rest of my music at Deerful.com, my Bandcamp or my Soundcloud.
